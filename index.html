AboutUs

Our company was founded in 2008. We operate three warehouses and a showroom of 3000m2 only a few kilometers away from Budapest, very convenient to access both by plane or car.

We sell leftover stocks, surplus stocks, sample collections of well-known fashion brands. All products are genuine, A-grade and brand new with their original tags on. We buy our garments directly from European distributors and always make sure that the quality of the products is impeccable.

Our range of products is growing wider continuously, we offer more than 60 well-known and popular brands to our partners. Our stock is renewed constantly, with daily new offers (you can subscribe to our newsletter). Our stocklots are predominantly made up of leftover or sample collections of popular and globally known fashion brands, however, at the same time we include more exclusive, less known, but excellent quality brands too.

Wholesalers and retailers both have the possibility to purchase from us, the minimum purchase quantity is 30 pieces. Thanks to our long-term, personal and international business relationships and connections, we are offering stocklots at the most advantageous prices.

Our team speaks Hungarian, Romanian, English, French, Arabic and Danish. We encourage you to come and visit us for a coffee, so you can see our showroom and current offers in person. We will help you arrange transportation, accommodation, should you decide to visit us. If needed, business advice and guidance is also offered by our experienced sales team, if you are new in the fashion industry.

We hope to see you soon!
